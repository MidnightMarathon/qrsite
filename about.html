QR Codes with No Watermark

Some QR code generators add a watermark or require sign-up. This one doesn’t. It runs in the browser, doesn’t track anything, and doesn’t add branding.

You can use it to make QR codes for URLs, plain text, and a few other formats. Everything stays local. Nothing gets uploaded or stored.

No logos. No accounts. Just clean QR codes.

Why No Watermark?

Watermarks take up space, distract from the content, and can make scanning less reliable. They’re not helpful if you just want to generate a code and use it however you want.

This tool leaves them out on purpose.
