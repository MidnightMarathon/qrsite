Why We Built OpenQR

Many QR code generators track your data, require signups, or add watermarks. We wanted a tool that would respect your privacy and be truly free to use.

OpenQR was created to give you full control over QR code creation — with no data collection, no tracking, and no fees. Your QR codes are generated entirely in your browser.

We believe privacy is a right, not a luxury. That’s why we built OpenQR with these core principles in mind.
